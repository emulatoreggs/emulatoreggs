On Linux, the official packaging method for Eden is an AppImage. 

To download Eden:

1. Open the Eden GitLab, [https://git.eden-emu.dev/eden-emu/eden/releases](https://git.eden-emu.dev/eden-emu/eden/releases) :material-information-outline:{ title="{{ steamdeckdesktopmode }}" }
2. Download the latest `Standard AppImage` appropriate for your system (if you are on a Steam Deck, download the Steam Deck version)
3. Move the downloaded AppImage to `$HOME/Applications`.
4. Right click the AppImage, {{ permissions }}
5. Double click the AppImage to run Eden. 
6. (Optional) For easier maintenance, rename the AppImage to `Eden.AppImage`
